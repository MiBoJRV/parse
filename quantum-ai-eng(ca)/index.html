    Sensational information! The Canadian government has given the green light for a new development by Elon Musk

    Quantum AI is the artificial intelligence of the future from Microsoft and OpenAI. At the end of 2015, businessman Elon Musk and investor Sam Altman founded OpenAI. Elon Musk is known for several projects at once: PayPal, SpaceX, and Tesla. And Sam Altman is known in narrower circles as the former director of the Y Combinator fund, who has invested in many startups.

    Quantum AI is the world's first artificial intelligence-based trading platform.

    The idea is simple: give ordinary people a chance to make money on the digital currency boom. Even if they have absolutely no experience in investing, trading or technology. Everyone knows that even having basic knowledge about trading is very difficult to resist the market and manipulators who provoke price hikes, accordingly it was decided to develop trading algorithm, based on artificial intelligence that would protect ordinary people from losing money on financial markets. People all over the world were shocked to hear about this new platform that promises to help you get rich fast.

    Quantum AI is a new platform for automated cryptocurrency trading which, according to Musk, can turn anyone into a millionaire in 3-4 months!

    Tesla calls this project "the biggest opportunity to make a small fortune quickly" and encourages people to try out their new platform before the global banking system tries to exert its influence.

    To scale the capabilities of artificial intelligence, Microsoft and Quantum AI began building a computing platform that could handle so much data. The result is a new Azure-based cloud supercomputer, which is one of the top five most powerful computers in the world in the TOP500 Supercomputers ranking.

    The supercomputer runs on 285,000 processor cores, 10,000 graphics blocks and a 1100 Gb/s network connection speed. This level of processing power is needed to process millions of texts, documents, photos and other information.

    We interviewed the technical director of Quantum AI, Ilya Sutzkever, so that he could better explain how Quantum AI works.

    Can you tell us how Quantum AI works?

    Quantum AI works as a team of hundreds of cities. We have millions of automated citizens in each city who go to the stock markets, they spend 12 hours a day collecting information. In the evening, these "cities" get together to exchange and compare what they have gathered. Then those we call the "kings of the cities" analyze all the information and try to find errors in the stock market; by finding the error, we can predict stock trends. Once we have a certain hot topic related to a major company, we analyze whether the trend is positive or negative.
    If the trend is positive, we bet on the stock going up; if the trend is negative, we bet on the stock going down. It's that simple.

    How often does Quantum AI get it wrong?

    Quantum AI computing is very different from everyday PCs. While a normal computer has one or two CPUs (processors), Quantum AI consists of tens of thousands of CPUs. If an error is made, the CPUs analyze the result and "vote" which decision is an error and which is the real deal. We are still in the beta testing phase of our computer and Quantum AI software, but it already shows more than 91% successful trades. This means that out of 100 trades that Quantum AI makes automatically, about 91 will be profitable.

    How much money can a typical Quantum AI user make?

    It depends a lot on the money they invest and the brokers they work with. Let's take the average user who starts with $350 and puts in $30 per trade. Within 24 hours, Quantum AI will make about 200 trades based on the information collected by Quantum AI. About 182 trades will be profitable, and 18 will not. This means that the user will earn about $4,480 and lose about $520. Exit at $3960 profit in one day. The broker adds small commissions, but they usually don't exceed 2%.

    Have any users actually lost their investment?

    No, we have never had a user who lost more than they invested. Even in the worst cases, we have always been profitable in the end.

    What improvements are you working on now?

    We are working on many of them right now, but Elon insisted that I not share them yet. Stay tuned, though it will be even better than it is now!

    How much will it cost to use an automated Quantum AI system?

    We don't know yet - it depends entirely on operating costs and where we want to use the technology. But the first 1500 seats will be free - we want people to help us test it (and make money in the process) . After that, for regular users, we're thinking of charging $12,000 a month and more than ten times that for those trading firms that want to use Quantum AI regularly in their business. We don't want to disrupt the stock market too much, so the spots will be limited to 500 trading firms and 50,000 regular users.

    When will Quantum AI be available?

    Very soon. We're just adding the finishing touches, and very soon the first participants will be available. First we will open the doors to small traders - regular people - and then to large trading firms. Again, the first 1,000 people will get a lifetime membership for free.
