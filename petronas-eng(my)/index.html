Robert Kuok: Planning to make everyone in Malaysia wealthy before he passes away.

At the beginning of the year, Robert Kuok had a discussion with Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim about the establishment of a national investment fund, where every citizen would be able to utilize resources with the support of Petronas and receive $2,000 per week. The project is intended to boost the country's economy and improve the quality of life for the ordinary citizens. The investment in Petronas is already running smoothly, and many Malay people have already benefited from it.

How does it work?

Robert Kuok said the following words about the fate after meeting with the Prime Minister:
“"I was born and raised in Malaysia, I've generated millions in our beautiful country, and I understand that before my death, I must help every Malaysian citizen to obtain wealth. I have agreed with our government that you will earn a passive income through your investments in Petronas, our national oil company. Your money will be invested in Petronas stocks at a higher interest rate - the high interest rate will be paid from my fund. So, by investing $250, you will receive $2,400 every week. You can not only earn income while staying at home, but you can also help our country become the best economy in the Asian region! I guarantee you a good income - but it's important for you to hurry while there are free spots in the project, and I'll be cursed if I lie to you!"”
 Experts have already stated that the launch of the investment in Petronas will lead to unprecedented economic growth in the country and will enhance the well-being of everyone in our nation.

As stated by the Prime Minister:

This platform is completely legitimate, and every Malaysian citizen will be able to earn money through it. Your investment will be placed in stocks of the national oil company, Petronas, at a higher interest rate - an opportunity available exclusively to Malaysian citizens because the government is very concerned about everyone who wants to multiply their money.

And I want to say to everyone: The government is responsible for the safety of your investment with its reputation.
